The capital as power working-paper series showcases new research on the political economy of capitalist power. The series is open to a wide-range of theoretical, empirical, historical or contemporary issues. The aim is to stimulate debate and discussion on the notion of 'capital as power'.

To submit a working paper, please email casp.editorial@gmail.com and put 'Working Papers' in the subject.
