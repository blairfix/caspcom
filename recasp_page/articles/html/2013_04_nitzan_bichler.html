Francis’ Buy-to-Build Estimates for Britain and the United States

A Comment

SHIMSHON BICHLER and JONATHAN NITZAN

October 2013

Abstract

Comments on Francis’ new estimates of the buy-to-build indicator for the United States and Britain. These estimates offer a welcome correction, modifications and additions to the U.S. numbers that we first presented in 1999 and later updated.

Keywords

Britain, buy-to-build indicator, merges and acquisitions, United States

Citation

Shimshon Bichler and Jonathan Nitzan (2013), ‘Francis’ Buy-to-Build Estimates for Britain and the United States: A Comment’, Review of Capital as Power, Vol. 1, No. 1, pp. 73-78.
